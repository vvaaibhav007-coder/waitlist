Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

We keep things simple. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

Introduction

Claritee is a decision intelligence app that helps users make structured, weighted decisions. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, who we share it with, and your rights.

Who We Are

  • App: Claritee
  • Website: clariteeai.me
  • Contact: support@clariteeai.me
  • Developer: Indie developer (solo)

What Data We Collect

a) Account Data

Email address, name (via Supabase authentication).

b) Decision Data

Decision questions, options, pros, cons, and weights that users enter inside the app.

c) Payment Data

Transaction records, credit purchase history. Raw card details are never stored — handled entirely by Dodo Payments.

d) Usage & Session Data

On clariteeai.me (main domain only), we use Microsoft Clarity for session recording and heatmaps to understand how users interact with the landing page. This does NOT apply to any subdomains or the app itself.

Why We Collect It

  • Account data: to authenticate users and identify their saved decisions.
  • Decision data: to deliver the core product — saving, scoring, and displaying decisions.
  • Payment data: to process credit purchases and maintain transaction records.
  • Session data (Microsoft Clarity): to improve the landing page experience.

Who We Share Your Data With

We work with four processors to run the app. Here is exactly what they receive and why.

Supabase

What they receive

Account data, decision data

Why

Database and authentication infrastructure

Google (Gemini API)

What they receive

Decision content sent for AI processing

Why

Powers AI-generated options, pros/cons, and explanations

Dodo Payments

What they receive

Payment and billing information

Why

Processes credit purchases

Microsoft (Clarity)

What they receive

Session recordings, heatmaps on clariteeai.me only

Why

Landing page analytics

Decision content sent to the Gemini API is used to generate AI responses. Please review Google's API Terms of Service for details on how API data is handled.

Data Retention

  • User data is retained for 2 years.
  • After 2 years, decision data is automatically deleted.
  • Account data is deleted when you delete your account.
  • Payment records may be retained longer where required by law.

Your Rights

  • Access your data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Export your decision history.
  • Opt out of session recording (by not using clariteeai.me — the main domain).
  • GDPR users: right to rectification, restriction of processing, and data portability.
  • To exercise any right, contact us at support@clariteeai.me.

Security

  • All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Authentication is handled by Supabase with industry-standard security.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party, ever.

Children

This app is not directed at users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of Clarity after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions: support@clariteeai.me
